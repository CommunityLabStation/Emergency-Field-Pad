COMMUNITY RESILIENCE PORTFOLIO
A living ecosystem of tools for discovery, preparedness, and collective care.

OVERVIEW
The Community Resilience Portfolio explores how communities can better understand their relationships, prepare for disruptions, and discover new opportunities for collaboration. It connects tools across community discovery, preparedness, pod mapping, collective care, and civic technology.
“Dreaming together is a form of preparedness.”

PROJECT VISION
What if communities could better understand their relationships, prepare for disruptions, and discover new opportunities for collaboration?
This portfolio treats dreaming, planning, and mapping as interconnected resilience practices.

CONNECTED PROJECT ECOSYSTEM

THE RESILIENCE TRIANGLE
Community Discovery — What are our needs?
Emergency Preparedness — What are our plans?
Pod Mapping — Who do we have?
These pillars form the Resilience Triangle, linking community, preparedness, and discovery into a unified model.

HOW TO USE THIS PORTFOLIO

Begin with Discovery to understand community needs.

Use Emergency Binder Prep to organize plans and readiness materials.

Map relationships using Pod Mapping to identify support networks.

Integrate tools to build a holistic resilience strategy.

Share and collaborate with community members, organizations, and networks.
